12 Color Types

Discover all color types in the Art Nouveau style!

Spring

Warm Spring
Light, warm complexion, golden hair, green or blue eyes.
Light Spring
Very light skin, blonde hair, light eyes. Pastel shades.
Clear Spring
Contrast between hair and skin. Clear, vivid colors.

Summer

Light Summer
Light complexion, ash hair, blue eyes. Cool pastels.
Cool Summer
Cool skin and hair. Cool, muted colors.
Soft Summer
Muted look, no contrasts. Soft colors.

Autumn

Warm Autumn
Warm, golden skin, red or brown hair. Earthy tones.
Deep Autumn
Deep look, dark hair and eyes. Saturated colors.
Soft Autumn
Muted look, shades of brown. Warm, soft colors.

Winter

Cool Winter
Light skin, dark hair, cool colors.
Deep Winter
Contrasting look. Deep, saturated colors.
Clear Winter
Light skin, dark hair. Clear, intense colors.
